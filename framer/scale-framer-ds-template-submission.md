# Scale Design System for Framer

A Framer Design System that helps individuals and organisations Scale. 

A new level of confidence and speed. Scale comes with industry standard foundations and components that help you create more consistent products, faster.

## What's Included

### 39 Components
Web components, Properties and Variants with thousands of possibilities.

### 120 Styles and Variables
Semantic Color and Type Styles that create a consistent design language across your entire project.

### 288 Icons
Feather icons and custom Status Icons to enhance your interfaces with clear, recognizable symbols.

### 15 Content Modules
Pre-built section blocks including Hero, Feature, Social, Pricing, CTA, and FAQ sections. These adaptive modules instantly inherit your global theme and resize perfectly for every device.

### 3 Page Templates
Launch-ready templates that help you skip "blank canvas" paralysis. Start with professional page structures, mix sections, apply your brand, and finish your site in a fraction of the time.

## Core Features

### Global Semantic Color
Use color in a meaningful way across your projects, toggle automatically between Light and Dark modes and watch updates ripple across every screen instantly. 

### Responsive Typography
Perfect hierarchy, zero math. Our t-shirt sized type scale uses fluid responsiveness to adapt flawlessly from Desktop to Mobile. No manual breakpoint tweaks required.

### Smart Component Library
Don't build from scratch, just configure. Access a robust library pre-wired with variants, variables and properties. Simply drag, drop, and toggle the props to match your needs.

### Adaptive Section Blocks
Drag-and-drop sections that instantly inherit your global theme and resize perfectly for every device. Edit the master component once, and see it update everywhere.

### Launch-Ready Templates
Skip "blank canvas" paralysis. Start with professional page structures. Mix sections, apply your brand, and finish your site in a fraction of the time.

## Why Choose Scale?

### Built for Speed
Scale comes with industry-standard components that make you create more consistent products, faster. Stop rebuilding the same patterns and focus on what makes your project unique.

### Confidence Through Consistency
Edit the master component once, and see it update everywhere. 
Your design system maintains perfect consistency across your 
entire project automatically.

### No Manual Tweaks
Our responsive system handles breakpoints automatically. Components adapt intelligently from Desktop to Mobile without manual adjustments.

### Professional Quality
Every component is production-tested and built to professional 
standards. Ship with confidence knowing your foundation is solid.
