## Licence Types

**Figma Licence** — Grants use of Scale's Figma design system in unlimited personal and commercial projects. You may modify Scale components as part of your project workflow. Redistribution or resale of Scale — in original or modified form — is strictly prohibited.

**Framer Licence** — Grants use of Scale's Framer design system in unlimited personal and commercial projects, including the creation of personal and client websites. You may not resell Scale as a Framer template or Design System template. For template creation licensing, contact us for custom pricing.

**Figma & Framer Licence** — Combines the Figma and Framer licences above under a single purchase.
