# Design Systems with Figma and Framer Versions

A curated list of design systems and UI kits that provide official versions for both Figma and Framer.

---

### [Cabana](https://cabanaui.com/)
A comprehensive design system with 1,000+ UI components and layout blocks built specifically for both Figma and Framer. Includes full variables support, dark/light mode, auto-layout, Tokens Studio optimization, and a library of 1,000+ icons.

**Pricing:** Framer only — $99 · Figma only — $109 · Both — $149 (one-time)

**Platform presentation:** Separate SKUs with a bundle option. The main landing page presents a unified product, but checkout offers three distinct purchase options.

---

### [DesignCode UI](https://designcodeui.com/)
An expansive design system with hundreds of components and templates, organized with variables, variants, and adaptive layouts. Components are built in Figma and ready to use in Framer.

**Pricing:** $120/year (subscription) · Team plans available at custom pricing · 30-day money-back guarantee

**Platform presentation:** Single unified product. Figma components are described as "ready for Framer" — framed as one system spanning both tools, with no platform separation.

---

### [Segment UI](https://segmentui.com/)
A broad collection of 1,000+ component variations, interactions, layouts, code overrides, and code components for both Framer and Figma. Positioned as a "360-degree solution" for either platform.

**Pricing:** ~$109 one-time (lifetime updates, no subscription)

**Platform presentation:** Separate pages, separate products. `/figmakit` and `/design-system` (Framer) are distinct landing pages sold independently.

---

### [Nova UI](https://novaui.design/)
A complete kit for both Framer and Figma with 400+ components, pre-built sections, and ready-to-use templates. Focused on modern marketing/website aesthetics.

**Pricing:** One-time payment (exact price not publicly indexed — check site directly)

**Platform presentation:** Single unified product. Both platforms listed together under one CTA — no separation between Figma and Framer.

---

### [Sprrrint](https://sprrrint.com/)
A component library and template collection with 300+ Framer and Figma components, updated daily. Covers common landing page sections (hero, pricing, FAQ, CTA, etc.) plus full website templates.

**Pricing:** Free tier available · Pro Plus — $9/month (includes all Figma + Framer components, gradients, templates, 100% commercial use)

**Platform presentation:** Shared brand, separate navigable sections. Dedicated URL paths for each platform (`/figma/components/all` and `/framer/sections/all`) — one site but the libraries are browsed independently.

---

### [GrayBlocks] https://grayblocks.net/
1,050+ UI blocks available for Figma, Framer, and Webflow. Focused on helping designers build premium websites quickly from pre-built responsive blocks.

**Pricing:** Free tier available · Pro plan available (exact price not publicly indexed — check [grayblocks.net/pricing](https://grayblocks.net/pricing) directly)

**Platform presentation:** Unclear from available data — likely filter-based given its component library nature, similar to Flowbase.

---

### [Koala UI](https://www.koalaui.com/)
A Figma-first design system also available for Webflow (note: primarily Figma + Webflow rather than Framer). Built for landing pages, marketing sites, SaaS dashboards, and AI-powered products. SEO and conversion-optimized component structure.

**Pricing:** Free (limited, 2,500+ components, 1 theme) · Individual — €124.99 one-time (5,000+ components, 4 themes, lifetime updates) · Agency — €554.40 one-time (up to 6 team members)

---

### [Flowbase](https://www.flowbase.co/)
One of the largest component libraries, covering Figma, Framer, and Webflow. Includes navbars, dashboards, filters, wireframes, and icons — suited for websites and web apps.

**Pricing:** Free (100+ components) · Pro+ — $39/month or $27/month billed annually · Monthly plan capped at 200 component copies/month; annual plan is unlimited

**Platform presentation:** Shared library with a platform filter. All components live together; a "Platform" filter narrows results to Webflow, Figma, or Framer. Each component card also lists which platforms it supports. Platform-specific browse URLs also available (`/components/platforms/figma`, etc.).

---

### [Clonify](https://clonify.design/)
Offers a plugin that works across both Figma and Framer, letting you drag UI assets directly onto your canvas without leaving your project. More of a component library than a full design system.

**Pricing:** Annual subscription or lifetime one-time payment available · Student/teacher 50% discount with proof (exact amounts not publicly indexed — check [clonify.io/pricing](https://clonify.io/pricing) directly)

**Platform presentation:** Plugin-based — no separate pages or filters needed. Assets are accessed from within whichever tool you're already using.
